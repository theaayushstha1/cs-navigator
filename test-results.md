## Q1: Prerequisite Chain (Academic Advisor)

**Query:** "What is the full prerequisite chain I need to complete before I can take COSC 457 Software Engineering?"

**Response:**

To take COSC 457 Software Engineering, you must complete COSC 336 Database Management with a grade of C or higher. The prerequisite for COSC 336 is COSC 220 Data Structures and Algorithms with a grade of C or higher. The prerequisite for COSC 220 is COSC 112 Introduction to Computer Science II with a grade of C or higher. The prerequisite for COSC 112 is COSC 111 Introduction to Computer Science I.

In summary, the full prerequisite chain for COSC 457 is:
1. COSC 111 - Introduction to Computer Science I
2. COSC 112 - Introduction to Computer Science II (Grade C or higher)
3. COSC 220 - Data Structures and Algorithms (Grade C or higher)
4. COSC 336 - Database Management (Grade C or higher)
